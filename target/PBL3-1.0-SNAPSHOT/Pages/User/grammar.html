Present Simple Tense

Structure

S + V(s/es) + O

Description

Used to describe habits, general truths, and regular actions.

Examples

  • She walks to school every day.
  • The sun rises in the east.

Analysis

  • Singular subject → Add "s" to verb
  • Describes regular actions

Memory Tip

"Present Simple – actions as regular as clockwork"

Exercises

1. She ___ (go) to school.

✅ Answer: b. goes
